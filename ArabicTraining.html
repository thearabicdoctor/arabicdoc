ARABIC TRAINING

Intensive language programs to cater for students of all levels.

As a very successful and innovative instructor of Arabic for more than twenty years, I offer intensive language programs to cater for students of all levels. My Certificate IV in Training and Assessment assists me in establishing your own learning requirements and providing you with the highest standard of teaching. With several years of hands-on experience in the Arabic speaking world I offer fun, dynamic and stimulating intensive language programs through which you will build a bank of grammar and vocabulary that serves you for life-long learning. I can offer courses in formal Arabic or several dialects, depending on your needs. My Arabic training assignments since leaving the university system include instructing at the Defence School of Languages, Victoria, and tutoring private courses for DFAT employees.
